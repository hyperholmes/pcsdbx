## Research Overview

**Strategic Value:** Essential for product stability and anti-aging formulations. Antioxidants protect formulations from oxidation and provide anti-aging benefits through free radical protection. Critical for SkinTwin formulation engine's ability to create stable, effective products.

**Key Specializations:**
- Vitamin E (tocopherols, tocotrienols)
- Vitamin C derivatives (ascorbic acid, sodium ascorbyl phosphate, MAP, ascorbyl palmitate)
- CoQ10 (ubiquinone, ubiquinol)
- Plant-based antioxidants (green tea, resveratrol, grape seed extract)
- Carotenoids (beta-carotene, astaxanthin, lycopene)
- Polyphenols and flavonoids
- Stability solutions and delivery systems
