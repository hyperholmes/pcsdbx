## Research Overview

**Strategic Value:** Popular active ingredient category essential for skin renewal and treatment formulations. High consumer demand for exfoliating products. Critical for SkinTwin's treatment product capabilities. AHA/BHA products are among the most popular skincare actives.

**Key Specializations:**
- AHA (Alpha Hydroxy Acids): glycolic acid, lactic acid, mandelic acid, citric acid
- BHA (Beta Hydroxy Acids): salicylic acid
- PHA (Polyhydroxy Acids): gluconolactone, lactobionic acid
- Fruit acid complexes (apple, grape, citrus)
- Enzyme exfoliants (papain, bromelain)
- Natural exfoliants (sugar, salt, jojoba beads)
- Physical exfoliants (microbeads alternatives)
- Concentration options and formulation support
