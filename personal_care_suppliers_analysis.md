## Website Purpose

The Personal Care Suppliers Directory is an online directory platform that serves as a comprehensive resource for the personal care products industry. It connects suppliers, manufacturers, and service providers within the cosmetics and personal care sector.

## Key Features

1. **Searchable Database:** Advanced search functionality with filtering options
2. **Category Browsing:** Organized by product and service categories
3. **Advertiser Listings:** Featured content and sponsored listings
4. **Association Badges:** Member verification system (Members, Vendor Showcase, Sponsors, Advertisers)
5. **Listing Management:** Options to create, edit, and update company listings

## Main Categories

The directory includes the following main categories:

- Accessories
- Business Services
- Equipment
- Labels / Sleeves
- Packaging (multiple subcategories):
  - Aerosol
  - Bags, Pouches
  - Bottles, Cans, Jars
  - Closures, Caps
  - Containers
  - Decorating
  - Fillers, Foam
  - Pumps, Sprayers
  - Secondary Packaging
  - Tubes
- Private Label Manufacturers
- **Raw Materials** (including Actives)
- Software
- Test Facilities / Consultants
- Testing Equipment

## Summary

The Personal Care Suppliers Directory serves as a comprehensive industry resource connecting raw material suppliers, manufacturers, equipment providers, and service companies within the personal care and cosmetics sector. The platform provides structured categorization, verified membership status, and multiple engagement options for both suppliers and buyers. The Actives category specifically lists 34 specialized suppliers of active ingredients, with a strong concentration in the United States and representation from major global chemical companies.
